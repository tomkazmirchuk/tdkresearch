Welcome

TDK Research & Consulting is a translational biomedical research group founded by Dr. Thomas Kazmirchuk. We develop artificial-intelligence–designed peptides to diagnose and correct dysfunctional blood clotting in rare and acquired bleeding disorders.

Our flagship program targets platelet-type von Willebrand disease (PT-VWD), a paradoxical bleeding disorder driven by gain-of-function mutations in the platelet receptor GPIbα.

Research Program

Blood clotting is governed by a network of protein–protein interactions that must be precisely balanced. We develop molecular tools that directly target disease-causing binding events.

In-Silico Protein Synthesizer (InSiPS)

InSiPS is an AI-guided peptide discovery platform that generates highly specific molecules that bind pathological protein interfaces with therapeutic precision.

Publications

Peer-reviewed research spanning hemostasis, peptide design, and molecular cell biology.
