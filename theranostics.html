Theranostics

Biomedical Research Division

The Theranostics division develops dual-function platforms that combine diagnostic insight with therapeutic action. This program is centered on AI-assisted peptide design, structural modeling, and experimental validation to generate molecules that both report on disease state and modulate pathological pathways.

Using in-silico design frameworks and structure-guided modeling, this platform targets protein–protein interactions involved in thrombosis, inflammation, and vascular dysfunction, enabling seamless transition from probe to lead.
