Molecular Biology

Biomedical Research Division

The Molecular Biology platform at TDK Research & Consulting provides the mechanistic foundation for all discovery programs, integrating molecular genetics, enzymology, and receptor biology with computational modeling and translational insight.

This division specializes in structure–function analysis, protein modification pathways, and pathway interrogation using model systems and engineered molecular tools.

Yeast Biology

Model systems for genetic manipulation, pathway dissection, and protein quality control, enabling rapid hypothesis testing and mechanism discovery.

Enzyme Biochemistry

Quantitative analysis of catalytic mechanisms, substrate specificity, and regulation using kinetic assays and structural modeling.

Receptor Ubiquitylation

Investigation of post-translational modification networks that control receptor signaling, trafficking, and degradation in health and disease.
